Visit Medellín

Experience the vibrant culture and stunning nature of this iconic city in the heart of Colombia.

Top three activities to do at Medellín

Visit the Pueblito Paisa

Take in panoramic views of Medellín from the top of Nutibara Hill while exploring this charming replica of a traditional Antioquian village.

Take a cultural walk through Medellín's historic downtown

Explore Medellín’s heart by visiting Plaza Botero with its famous sculptures, stroll through the lively Parque Berrio, walk along the historic Junín Street, and end at the nostalgic Salón Málaga, a café rich in tango history and local culture.

Enjoy a football match at Atanasio Girardot Stadium

Experience the passion for football firsthand by attending a match at the home of local teams Atlético Nacional (the most successful and biggest Colombian team) or Independiente Medellín.
